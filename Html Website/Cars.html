TOP 3 CARS FOR RANKING UP YOUR LEVEL

  • OCTANE
  • DOMINUS
  • FENNEC
  • NIMBUS
  • BREAKOUT

    • OCTANE

    The Octane is the default car that players start with when they first begin playing Rocket League. Unsurprisingly, it uses the Octane Hitbox. The Octane has become a favorite among Rocket League players and has often been the go-to Body by professional players in esports competitions.

    DOMINUS

    The Dominus is a vehicle released on August 13, 2015, which could only be obtained as DLC from the Supersonic Fury DLC Pack before it was made unlockable for free without purchase. The Dominus hitbox, used by other cars, is based off of it.

    FENNEC

    The Fennec car is a vehicle that you can equip in your Rocket League garage which uses the Octane hitbox. The car itself was added to the game in July 2019 and has been an instant hit among players of all ranks.

    NIMBUS

    Boasting an appealing design and the highly sought-after Hybrid Hitbox, the Nimbus offers a unique blend of speed, control, and versatility on the field. Whether you're dribbling the ball with finesse or soaring through the air for that game-winning aerial shot, the Nimbus delivers an unrivaled experience.

    BREAKOUT

    Breakout is a battle-car released on July 7, 2015, with the release of the game. It can be unlocked by completing matches. It is classified as "Common" rarity. Since August 3, 2017, painted variants of Breakout are available through the trade-in system.
